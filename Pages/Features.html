Features

Singphony is an open-source, web-based music composition tool. The aim of Singphony is to allow anyone the opportunity to create music without the use of a conventional instrument.

To use Singphony all you need is your voice! Sing, hum or whistle whatever tune is in your head and Singphony will use digital signal processing algorithms to extract the melody from your recording and display it on screen.

You can add up to 4 tracks which can be played back in parallel using a variety of different synthesised sounds by way of an oscillator. The sound of the oscillator can be varied by selecting different wave and filter types.

Your creations can be saved as CSV files or PNG images.
